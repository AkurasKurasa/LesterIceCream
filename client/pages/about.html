ABOUT US

LESTER ICE CREAM

"Lester Ice Cream" is a modest and unpretentious ice cream shop, celebrated for its focus on simple, high-quality offerings. The essence of the shop is its straightforward approach to serving ice cream. It features a concise menu that highlights a selection of classic flavors, each made with care and attention to quality ingredients.

There are no elaborate presentations or gourmet concoctions; instead, the shop focuses on delivering consistently delicious, comforting ice cream that speaks for itself. It's this dedication to the basics of good ice cream and genuine service that defines Lester Ice Cream's identity, making it a beloved and enduring establishment in its community.
"It's funny where a little hard work can take you."
Devasted by the Mount Pinatubo Eruption in 1991.

Mt. Pinatubo's eruption in 1991 stands as a significant natural disaster in the Philippines, profoundly affecting local businesses, including the cherished Lester Ice Cream Shop.

This once-thriving establishment, known for its delightful array of flavors, faced devastating losses due to the eruption. The ashfall and subsequent lahar flows not only damaged the physical structure of the shop but also disrupted the local economy, posing challenges to its operations and survival in the wake of this natural calamity.

Relocated in a small town in Mabalacat
called Madapdap.

Nestled in the heart of Mabalacat, Pampanga, Madapdap is a quaint small town that boasts a rich cultural heritage and a strong sense of community.

Known for its resilient spirit, Madapdap has seen significant transformations over the years, particularly following the eruption of Mt. Pinatubo. Despite these challenges, the town has maintained its charming character, with vibrant local businesses and a community that is deeply interconnected and supportive of one another.

We built our business one step at a time.

Lester Ice Cream began as a family-owned venture, rooted in a passion for creating homemade, artisanal ice cream.

The founder, inspired by childhood memories of enjoying ice cream with family, sought to recreate those moments of joy and share them with the community. Starting with a small selection of classic flavors, the shop quickly gained popularity for its rich, creamy textures and innovative blends, becoming a beloved local treasure in the heart of Madapdap.

And soon we were able to able to bring smiles to everyone.

Over the years, Lester Ice Cream has become synonymous with joy and community spirit in Madapdap.

Its delightful range of flavors and warm, welcoming ambiance have been a source of happiness for both locals and visitors. The shop, with its colorful decor and friendly staff, has been a gathering place for families and friends, a site of cherished memories, and a testament to the simple pleasure of enjoying a scoop of delicious ice cream.

And support our family.

Beyond serving delectable ice cream, Lester Ice Cream has played a vital role in supporting the founder's family and contributing to the local community.

As a family-run business, it has provided livelihoods and fostered a sense of pride and belonging among its members. The shop has also engaged in community activities, sponsoring local events and collaborating with other small businesses, thereby reinforcing its commitment to the growth and well-being of Madapdap.

FAQs

What if I am allergic to a certain ingredient ?

If you're allergic to an ingredient in our ice creams, inform our staff. We will guide you to suitable alternatives or flavors that do not contain the allergen, ensuring your safety and enjoyment.

Do you have ice cream for those who are lactose intolerant ?

Yes, we are pleased to offer a variety of lactose-free ice cream options catering to those who are lactose intolerant. Our aim is to ensure that everyone can enjoy the delightful experience of indulging in ice cream.

Do you make custom ice-cream flavors ?

Yes, we can create custom ice cream flavors upon request. Please contact us in advance to discuss your preferences and any specific requirements.

Do you have any gluten-free options ?

No, at present, we do not have gluten-free options in our ice cream selection. We understand the growing need for dietary-specific offerings and are actively considering expanding our menu to include gluten-free choices in the future.

Do you deliver outside of Pampanga ?

We are currently exploring options to expand our delivery services beyond Pampanga. While we don't deliver outside the area at the moment, we hope to offer this service in the near future.
